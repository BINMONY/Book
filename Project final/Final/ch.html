Chanel

Gabrielle Chanel cultivated the art of paradox. The woman behind an understated, pared-down style that revolutionized women’s fashion was equally fond of the baroque, which inspired the decor of her apartment on rue Cambon and her Byzantine jewelry collections. COCO expresses the intensity of Mademoiselle’s personality. A mysterious, provocative ambery scent that gradually reveals its contrasting notes.
